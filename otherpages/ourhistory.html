Agriculture

We have been committed to environmentally friendly agriculture from 2014. We have 45 ha of cashew nuts farm called “SCY (Sambor Community of Youth)”, which is located near Sambor Prei Kuk, registered as World Heritage in 2017. We don’t use any pesticides nor chemical fertilizer, but we take care of cashew nuts in ecological ways, in order that we keep natural life for the future. Students in Phum Asia and SCY practices the ecological way and learn how to proceed to final products. Phum Asia believes in environmentally friendly cultivation of food to ensure a sustainable future.
